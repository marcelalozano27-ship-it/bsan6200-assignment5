
The role

We are looking for an intern to work on financial strategy at Ephemeris Net. You will work with the Head of Government Programs & Finance on a project that directly supports how we differentiate ourselves, close customers, or make strategic decisions.

Ephemeris Net is building the networking layer for the space economy. Getting the business model, financial projections, and commercial positioning right is just as important as getting the hardware right. You will help us build financial strategy that drive the business forward.

By the end of this internship, you will have delivered work that directly supports growth at a space infrastructure company. Your specific project will depend on your background and interests. We will match you to a project that is both meaningful to the company and a strong learning opportunity for you.

Example Projects

Upcoming intern projects span three areas. You would work on one of these tracks, not all three:

Valuation & Modeling (VC Fundraise)

Explore non-dilutive and hybrid financing instruments relevant to Ephemeris's stage and sector: SBIR/STTR stacking, DoD OTA agreements, export credit facilities for international customers, debt, etc.
Build or refine financial models supporting VC fundraise: unit economics, revenue projections, scenario analysis, and cap table modeling
Prepare and iterate on pitch deck materials and investor-facing documentation
Manage logistics and coordination for fundraise processes: data room organization, investor outreach tracking, and meeting preparation

Commercial & Government Deals (Business Development)

Conduct market mapping and competitive analysis: identify target customer segments, adjacent markets, and strategic partnership opportunities 
Build pricing models and business case analyses for new product offerings or customer engagements
Conduct pricing research, rate analysis, and volume/cost modeling to support pricing decisions

Partnership Strategy (Supply Chain Support)

Conduct pricing research, rate analysis, and volume/cost modeling to support make-versus-buy decisions and supplier negotiations 
Audit current and planned supplier relationships across SRT and LRT BOMs and propose opportunities to de-risk the program
Directly negotiate six figure procurement deals, balancing vendor relationship development, program de-risking, and financial success of the company.

What You Will Learn

How commercial space deals get done: from customer discovery and proposal development to pricing and contract structure 
How market mapping and competitive intelligence translate into go-to-market strategy 
How to effectively translate between technical and business stakeholders to create a compelling product
How to work as part of a small, focused team where your contributions directly affect the company’s trajectory

Ability to work independently on a scoped project with mentorship and regular check-ins

How This Role Works

While you will have direct interfaces with the CEO and technical team, you will likely (project pending) report to the Head of Government Programs & Finance, who will serve as your primary mentor. You will have regular check-ins and direct access to them for guidance.
Before you start, we will meet to understand your strengths and growth goals and scope a project for you.
You are expected to scope your project, execute, and present your results to the team at the end of the internship.
