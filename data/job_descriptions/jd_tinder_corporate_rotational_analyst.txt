

Match Group is launching a Corporate Rotational Analyst Program, a two-year, cohort-based program designed to develop high-performing early-career talent into well-rounded business leaders. Analysts will rotate through four core functions across finance, strategy, product marketing and product, gaining hands-on experience with the strategic, analytical, and operational drivers of our business.


The first cohort will begin within Tinder, allowing participants to develop a deep understanding of a single business while benefiting from strong cohort learning, mentorship, and leadership visibility. Upon successful completion of the program, Analysts will transition into full-time roles within Match Group.



Rotational teams may include:

- Corporate Finance / Analytics: Financial planning, forecasting, budgeting, reporting, and performance analysis
- Corporate Development / Strategy: Market assessment, valuation, strategic analysis, and M&A support
- Product Management: Product strategy, roadmap development, feature prioritization, and data-driven decision-making
- Product Marketing: Go-to-market strategy, positioning, market research, storytelling, and campaign analytics


Each Analyst will complete two business-focused rotations and two product-focused rotations, ensuring balanced exposure and transferable skill development.


What You’ll Do
Analyze complex business and product problems and develop actionable insights
Build and interpret financial, strategic, and analytical models
Partner cross-functionally with leaders in finance, strategy, product, and marketing
Contribute to high-impact projects that influence company strategy and performance
Present findings and recommendations to senior stakeholders
Develop a strong understanding of Match Group’s brands, growth levers, and financial architecture


Qualifications
Currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree with graduation by Spring 2026
Strong analytical, problem-solving, and quantitative skills
Ability to communicate clearly and effectively with diverse stakeholders
Interest in technology, consumer products, and business strategy


Preferred Qualifications
Majors in Economics, Finance, Business, Statistics, Data Science, Engineering, or related fields
Internship experience in consulting, banking, analytics, strategy, product, or technology
Demonstrated ownership, curiosity, and ability to thrive in fast-paced environments
