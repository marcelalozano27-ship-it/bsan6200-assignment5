
Roku’s global Content team is responsible for sourcing and executing innovative content licensing and production deals across a range of content types and business models for The Roku Channel (TRC) AVOD and FAST live/linear service and Howdy SVOD service across US and international territories. 


About the role 
Roku is looking for a Content Strategy & Insights MBA Intern to help shape the content strategy for The Roku Channel (AVOD, FAST), Howdy (SVOD). In this role, you will use Roku’s first-party data and business intelligence tools to answer high-impact questions about what content works where, why, and how we should invest next. 

You will work closely with Content Acquisition, Content Partner Management, Originals, Sports, Finance, and Core Analytics teams to own project-based analyses from problem definition through recommendations. Rather than building complex models or writing production code, you will focus on pulling and structuring data from dashboards, analyzing it, and translating your findings into clear stories that inform real content decisions. 

This is a full-time summer internship designed for an MBA student who is passionate about content, eager to dive deep into data, and excited to influence how millions of people discover and watch TV. 



What you’ll be doing 
Pull and synthesize content performance, audience, and engagement data from Tableau, Looker, and internal dashboards for TRC AVOD, FAST channels, and Howdy in the US and Mexico 
Analyze viewership, engagement, and monetization trends to identify what types of content, formats, and windowing strategies are working across AVOD, FAST, and SVOD 
Build clear, executive-ready narratives that translate complex data into simple, persuasive stories and recommendations 
Partner with Licensed and Original Content teams to scope project-based work, align on hypotheses, and pressure-test your findings 
Proactively surface new questions and opportunities where data-driven insights can improve slate planning, scheduling, and monetization
